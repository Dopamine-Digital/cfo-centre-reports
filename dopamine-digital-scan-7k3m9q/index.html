3 structural areas where profit is most likely slipping

Based on what is publicly observable about how Dopamine Digital operates. A 20 minute call turns this into specifics.

Where the leaks tend to hide

  • Pricing & packaging — recurring revenue priced on effort, not on the value delivered.
  • Delivery cost drift — fulfilment hours creeping up per account without a margin check.
  • Cash timing — the gap between when costs are paid and when revenue actually lands.

The 3 we would tighten first for Dopamine Digital

  • Re-anchor the core offer to a value metric so price rises with the outcome.
  • Put a simple per-account margin line in front of delivery before scope expands.
  • Pull cash forward with billing timing, not discounts.
